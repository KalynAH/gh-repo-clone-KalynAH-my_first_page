~About Us~

Gateway 5 Bar and Grill, opened this past Summer of 2018. The location is near the EGCC Youngstown's campus. The bar and grill was started as a place to cheer on the schools baseball team while creating a safe and enjoyable place for students to eat, drink socialize and congregate between classes or after class. The eatery will only offer food and drinks that are five dollars and below. We also have a Brunch on Sundays only. All Patrons must be 21 and over.

Enjoy entrees and drinks all under $5.00!
